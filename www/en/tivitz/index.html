    • Tivitz

    3D Engine Developer

    An on-line 3D game helping children learn math.

    Tivitz.com is the digital transposition of a popular USA board game, it allows children to play the board game on-line against their friends around the world. Create your avatar, customize it, register the site and find opponents to play with.
